TRIBUTE TO A LOVED ONE

Like a sapling we grew together, hoping and wishing for better days to come. We built our perfect life's in our imaginations and drafted the future in illusions, you were a source of inspiration and light to many people. Words cannot really tell of how you loved and cared for others through your act of selflessness. Throughout your journey on earth, you were a calm person, but in that calm nature of yours, you instilled joy and happiness to everyone who crossed your path.

Dear Olofu, it's been a year since you walked this earth, I sometimes still imagine you on earth, but guess what, they are all imaginations. I won't get to see those beautiful smile of yours that gave people hope. Your death struck a blow, one that left many in pain and grief, although as the saying goes, Time heals, am still in the healing process and I believe all will be alright. The short life you lived here on earth was indeed a remarkable one, one which will not be forgotten in a haste.

I believe I'll see you on the other side, where heaven and earth collide, I can hear the angels singing the sweetest lullaby, I know I'll see you on the other side. Heaven knows how had it is, but I won't give up on love

You were indeed a calm person.
Rest on dear friend, till we meet to part no more.



Written by Anene Mary-Cynthia Adizie.
Nigeria.
